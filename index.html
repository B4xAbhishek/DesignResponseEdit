Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions, we have answers.

How many videos can I get in a month?

It depends on the complexity and duration of the videos requested. Typically, for the 24/7 subscription, we deliver 2-3 short-form videos every day (yes, including weekends). However, for certainty, please book a call with us and we can give you a more customised estimate.

Can you chop up shorter clips from our existing content on Youtube or our backlog of videos?

Yes, our team of content writers also work with your editors (behind the scenes), to help pick the best short-form videos from your longer videos so that you can post more short-form videos across all socials! (NOTE: this feature is only available on the 24/7 non-stop creatives plan.)

How much detailed instructions do we need to give for the videos to be made?

The more detailed, obviously the better. However, most of our clients just send us the raw footage, the copywriting and the reference. We’ll take it from there and work our magic!

Do we need to have 1 subscription for each brand we need videos for?

No, you don’t! With 1 subscription, you can request videos/graphics for as many brands as you want.

What timezone does the editors work in?

For the 9-5 subscription, they work in your preferred timezone, wherever you are. For the 24/7 subscription, their timezone wouldn’t matter because you’ll always have an editor ready on standby for you.

Can you do subtitles and are there caption checkers that double check before sending to us?

Yes, we can do English subtitles and we have professional caption checkers that will double check all the captions, including the punctuations if requested. This comes at no extra cost for all packages.

Are the b-rolls you provide all commercially usable?

Yes, we usually insert b-rolls from stock footage platforms that give commercial rights.

How does the guaranteed same-day turnaround for the 24/7 work?

It's guaranteed for vertical videos under 60-seconds with minimal animations - captions, simple animations & rough cut included
